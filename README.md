# 🌞 Cardinal

> Understand your home. Not your sensors.

Cardinal is an intelligent energy application for Home Assistant.

Rather than displaying raw sensor values and graphs, Cardinal explains what is happening in your home using simple language, beautiful visualisations and meaningful insights.

Whether you're generating solar, charging a battery, importing from the grid or exporting energy, Cardinal aims to answer the questions people naturally ask:

* Where is my energy coming from?
* Where is it going?
* How much did I save today?
* How much did I earn from exporting?
* Is today better than yesterday?
* What is likely to happen next?

## Vision

Home Assistant gives us incredible data.

Cardinal turns that data into understanding.

## Goals

* Live energy flow
* Daily timelines
* Cost and profit tracking
* Battery and solar analytics
* Historical insights
* Forecasting and predictions
* Simple, beautiful user experience

## Philosophy

Every screen should answer a question.

Every number should have meaning.

Complexity should always be optional.
