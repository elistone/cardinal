# Vision

## Mission

Cardinal helps people understand their home.

Rather than displaying raw sensor values, graphs and technical metrics, Cardinal transforms Home Assistant data into meaningful insights, explanations and predictions.

The goal is simple:

> Understand your home. Not your sensors.

## What is Cardinal?

Cardinal is a Home Assistant application focused on energy intelligence.

It combines data from solar, batteries, the electrical grid, weather forecasts and energy tariffs to explain what is happening inside a home.

Rather than asking users to interpret dozens of graphs, Cardinal answers questions like:

* Where is my electricity coming from?
* Where is it going?
* How much have I saved today?
* How much have I earned from exporting?
* Why is today different from yesterday?
* What is likely to happen next?

## Long Term Vision

Cardinal should become the operating system for the modern smart home.

Energy is only the first module.

Future modules may include:

* Water
* Gas
* EV Charging
* Heating
* Air Quality
* Environmental Monitoring
* Appliance Intelligence

The architecture should be designed so that additional modules can be added without redesigning the application.

## Design Goals

Cardinal should be:

* Beautiful
* Fast
* Mobile-first
* Simple by default
* Powerful when explored
* Accessible
* Data driven

## Success

A successful Cardinal interface should allow a user to understand the current state of their home within five seconds.

If a graph needs explaining, the interface has failed.
